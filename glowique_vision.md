# Glowique Skincare - Brand Vision & Products

## Brand Positioning & Tone
* **Brand Name**: Glowique
* **Positioning**: Premium, clean, dermatologist-backed skincare brand.
* **Tone**: Elegant, trustworthy, and modern, targeting individuals who want effective, scientific, yet luxurious skincare.
* **Color Theme**: Rose pink, white, gold, and dark grey.
* **Feel**: Luxurious but approachable, medical-grade efficacy combined with clean beauty aesthetics.

## Website Structure (Multi-page)
1. **Home Page**:
   - **Hero Section**: High-end banner featuring clean skin, liquid drops of serums, gold and rose pink accents. Hook: *"Science-Backed Radiance. Your Skin's New Era."*
   - **Brand Story**: Brief introduction on Glowique’s philosophy (Clean, Effective, Backed by Science).
   - **Best Sellers Section**: Grid of top products (Moisturizer, Serum, Sunscreen).
   - **Skincare Quiz Banner**: An interactive block urging users to find their perfect routine.
   - **Customer Testimonials**: Elegant slider.
2. **Shop / Products Page**:
   - **Filter options**: By product type (Face wash, moisturizer, serum, sunscreen, toner, exfoliating peel, eye cream) or by Skin Concern (Acne, Aging, Dryness, Glow).
   - **Product Grid**: Clean product cards with hover effects (showing active ingredients like Niacinamide, Hyaluronic Acid, etc.).
   - **Direct WhatsApp Ordering**: Every product card and details page has an "Order via WhatsApp" button. Redirects to `+91 88377 41698` with a pre-filled message (product name and price).
3. **About Us / Our Philosophy Page**:
   - **"The Science of Glow"**: Highlighting key ingredients we use (Hyaluronic acid, Retinol, Vitamin C, Centella Asiatica).
   - **Our Commitment**: Cruelty-free, paraben-free, dermatologist tested.
4. **Skincare Quiz Page (Interactive)**:
   - 4-5 engaging questions (Skin type, main concern, daily sun exposure, current routine).
   - Recommends personalized Glowique routine (e.g. Cleanser + Toner + Serum + Moisturizer) with an "Order this routine via WhatsApp" button.
5. **Contact Us Page**:
   - Elegant contact form.
   - Direct "Chat on WhatsApp" button for immediate consultations.
   - Location/Support hours.
